# Grounded Writing Checklist

- Did the section draw primarily from full-text notes rather than only abstracts?
- Did the subsection follow the refined outline hierarchy?
- Did the subsection retrieve and use local evidence before drafting?
- If the subsection was thin, was there a supplemental retrieval or repair pass?
- Is every substantive claim supported by at least one source?
- Are strong claims supported by strong sources?
- Are disagreements or uncertainty stated clearly?
- Does each paragraph stay on one comparison axis?
- Are citations attached near the claims they support?
- Did we avoid writing beyond the evidence?
- Is the section understandable without inflating confidence?
