# Screening Checklist

Use this checklist during title and abstract screening.

## Include when

- The paper studies memory in agents or agent-like LLM systems.
- The paper is directly relevant to personalization, user modeling, profile memory, preference memory, or long-term adaptation.
- The paper is directly relevant to procedural memory, skill memory, reflective memory, or reusable action knowledge.
- The paper contributes a taxonomy, benchmark, survey, or strong empirical comparison.

## Exclude when

- The paper is only about generic RAG without an agent-memory contribution.
- The paper studies only base-model parametric memory with no agent setting.
- The paper is purely application-specific and does not contribute reusable memory design insight.
- The paper is clearly superseded and no longer useful as a representative source.

## Record during screening

- decision: include / maybe / exclude
- reason
- theme tags
- whether full-text reading is required
